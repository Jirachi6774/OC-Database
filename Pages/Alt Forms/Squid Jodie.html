Squid Jodie
イカジョディエ

Description
Squid Jodie is a Cosmic Colored Squid often called as a Woomy.
Her skin is smooth but sticky, only has 2 big tentacles and 4 tinies at her bottom area, her mouth is also behind those tiny tentacles on her bottom.
